Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to NeuroComet. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, secure digital environment tailored to the neurodivergent community. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications (Android/iOS) and website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect authentication data via our backend provider (Supabase), which may include your email address and profile credentials.
  • App Activity & Preferences: We store your UI theme preferences (Calm, Focus, Energy), "Spoon" energy tracking logs, and app settings locally on your device to ensure a consistent experience.
  • Social Feed Data: Posts, stories, and interactions within the community feed are stored securely on our servers. This data is subject to rigorous automated Content Filtering.

3. Gemini AI Mock Call Simulator (Audio Data)

Our Mock Call Practice feature utilizes WebRTC and Google's Gemini AI. When using this feature:

  • Microphone access is required locally on your device.
  • Voice data is processed in real-time to facilitate the AI conversation.
  • We do not record, store, or sell your voice data. Transcripts or audio from practice calls are transient and strictly used to simulate the conversational experience.

4. Parental Controls & Caregiver Features

NeuroComet includes optional linked accounts for caregivers. If Parental Controls are activated, a designated caregiver may have visibility into specific app usages or the ability to restrict certain content/DMs to protect the primary user from harm or overstimulation.

5. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and personal data at any time. This can be done directly within the app under Settings > Privacy & Security > Account Care. When you request deletion:

  • Your account is deactivated and scheduled for deletion after a 14-day grace period.
  • During these 14 days, you can cancel the request by signing back in.
  • After 14 days, all your data is permanently removed from our production databases.

For more details, please visit our Account Deletion Page.

6. Data Security & Content Filtering

We use end-to-end security utility frameworks to protect your data. Furthermore, our community guidelines are enforced by strict Direct Message (DM) privacy filters and trigger-word blocking to maintain a safe ecosystem. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us via our GitHub Repository or official support channels.
