## Full Description (Max 4,000 chars)

**Welcome to NeuroComet – A Safe Space for Every Mind ✨**

NeuroComet is a social platform built from the ground up for neurodivergent (ADHD, Autism, Dyslexia, Anxiety, and more) and LGBTQ+ individuals. We believe that every brain is beautiful and deserves a digital environment that adapts to its unique needs, rather than forcing you to mask.

**YOUR BRAIN, YOUR WAY**
Stop fighting against overwhelming layouts. NeuroComet offers unprecedented visual control:
*   🎨 **25+ Neuro-State Themes:** Choose specialized themes for ADHD focus, Autism sensory comfort, Anxiety relief, and Colorblind accessibility.
*   📖 **Accessible Reading:** 12+ neuro-friendly fonts including OpenDyslexic, Lexend, and Atkinson Hyperlegible. Adjust spacing, weight, and size to fit your reading style.
*   ♾️ **Calm Mode:** Instantly reduce visual motion and distracting effects with a single tap of our infinity symbol.
*   🌙 **True Dark & High Contrast:** Maximum visibility modes for low-vision and light-sensitive users.

**A SUPPORTIVE COMMUNITY**
Connect with people who "get it." Whether you’re sharing your latest hyperfixation or looking for executive function hacks, you belong here.
*   🤝 **Safe Communities:** Join dedicated spaces for ADHD Hacks, Sensory Tips, Late Diagnosis support, and LGBTQ+ Pride.
*   💬 **Quiet Messaging:** Connect via DMs with readable layouts and safety-forward privacy controls.
*   🌈 **Inclusive by Design:** A platform where LGBTQ+ and neurodivergent identities intersect and are celebrated.

**BUILT-IN WELLBEING TOOLS**
We prioritize your mental health over "engagement" metrics:
*   🔋 **Energy & Social Battery Trackers:** Manage your "spoons" with home screen widgets.
*   🧘 **Sensory Regulation:** Access built-in "NeuroGames" like Bubble Pop and Zen Sand for calming stim breaks.
*   🛡️ **Safety & Privacy:** Robust content filtering, parental controls with PIN protection, and bedtime modes to help you unplug.
*   🏆 **Earn Badges:** Celebrate your journey with achievements for community participation and self-care.

**PRIVACY-FORWARD & AD-FREE OPTIONS**
Your data is encrypted and secure. Choose an experience that respects your attention—support our development with a premium subscription to remove ads and unlock exclusive themes.

Join thousands of others in the space where you can finally stop masking and start connecting.

**NeuroComet: Calm, clear, and ready when you are. 💜**

## Feature Graphic Design (1024x500)

I have created two ways for you to generate the mandatory 1024x500 feature graphic:

### Option 1: Jetpack Compose (Highest Fidelity)
Open `FeatureGraphicPreview.kt` in Android Studio. You can use the **Compose Preview** to see the design and take a high-resolution screenshot directly from the IDE.

### Option 2: Python Script (Quick Generation)
Run the following script in your terminal to generate a `feature_graphic.png` file:
```bash
pip install Pillow
python tools/generate_feature_graphic.py
```
